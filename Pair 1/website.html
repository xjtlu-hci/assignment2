Welcome to our studio

HANDICRAFT is a product design studio founded by university students. The studio started in March, 2017.

HANDICRAFT produce pure handmade product including wooden furniture model and chinaware. Our design puts

the idea on "human-centered" into basic practice of " humanized design. Customers can provide blueprints

or ideas, and we will help design products. This is a students' innovative undertaking. We will do our

best to give you the best services.
