ABOUT KANAHEI

Kanahei’s animals are cartoon characters that created by a Japanese cartoonist named Kanahei.
Soon after they were created, many young girls become fan of these cute drawings.
They are popular for their unique style and soft color.


ABOUT US

Our studio designs products based on Kanahei’s animals.
We not only design products for daily use such as bags and toys, but also try to make many digital projects.
To know more about our products, you can click ‘Design Projects’.
