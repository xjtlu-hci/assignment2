Leo's Project:
Design for air-hostess

This is the trolly designed for
air-hostess for a better working experience.
The product mainly improved two parts

  • Water pouring system
  • Breaking system

It could change the way
airhostess pouring the water from
lifiting the bottle to press a button.
The right picture is the sketch of the trolly.

It changes the foot-stepped break
into a hand-pushed break, which can
prevent airhostess from tiring ankles and
makeg a safer dlivery by combining
pushing and break together.
