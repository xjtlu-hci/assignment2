Nonexistent studio is a studio made up of two people aimed at creating elegant, imaginative illustration and graphic design.

Didot is currently in charge of graphic design of our studio and David is the illustration designer in the studio. We intend to combine illustration and graphic together and create graphic works with strong taste of illustration. We understand graphic design needs to be inspired by the spirit of art to keep its elegance. Our expertise lies in black and white illustration, poster design and visual communication.
