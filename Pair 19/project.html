Project 2

Description:This product order to help the doctor to wash the slide in hospital. It is made by acrylic board shell and plastic water pipe. How did it work? It has two part, one part is wash machine, and the other part is make the slides to dry after washing. The wash part has a shelf to put slides, and a pipe on the top of shelf which can wash every slide accurately. And the dirty water will be put out on the bottom. The dry part is a large shelf, user can put the slides which had washed on it.
